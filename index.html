Common questions

Is using a professional mortgage service free for borrowers? Many professional mortgage services in Brisbane operate as free comparison platforms for borrowers. These services typically receive commissions from lenders when loans settle, meaning borrowers do not pay direct fees for the comparison service. However, borrowers should always confirm the fee structure before proceeding.

How long does the home loan application process take? The timeline varies depending on loan type, lender, and borrower circumstances. Pre-approval can sometimes be obtained within a few days, while full approval and settlement typically take several weeks. Having all documentation ready and responding quickly to lender requests helps expedite the process.

What documents do I need for a home loan application? Required documents typically include identification, proof of income (payslips or tax returns), bank statements showing savings history, details of existing debts and assets, and information about the property being purchased. Self-employed borrowers may need additional financial documentation.
